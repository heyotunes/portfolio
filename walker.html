PROJECT OVERVIEW

WALKER

Walker is an E-Commerce responsive website for sneakerheads. During the building process, I started by doing research on what other websites in the sneaker industry were doing. I looked at which features they had, what their design was like, and how many products they had listed. Then I compared those features to the features I wanted for my website. I created a list of all of the features that I wanted to have on my website, as well as a list of all of the features that I knew from my research were important for people who owned sneaker stores or worked in the sneaker industry. I took inspiration from similar platforms like Nike, Addidas, Goat etc. which are similar, in terms of running a successful sneaker website. The process was so much simpler than I expected and the end result was a truly unique store that functioned exactly how I wanted it to.
