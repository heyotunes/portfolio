HOW IT WORKS

Good Eats

Good Eats is a simple and easy-to-use app that allows users to search for restaurants in their area and also save their favourite restaurants with one click. The app is pretty simple: you put in your location, and it'll show you all of the restaurants within a certain distance from there. It's perfect for people who are looking for somewhere new to eat or just want to try out something new in their own neighborhood. For This project I created an algorithm using Google places Api that compares your location to restaurants' locations and reviews, then shows you the results. I also used firebase to create a login page for each users so they can have a intimate experience with the app
