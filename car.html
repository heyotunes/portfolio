PROJECT OVERVIEW

FASTCARZ

Fastcarz is a landing page I created from scratch for luxury car rentals. The rental car website is the perfect place to find a great deal on your next car rental. With so many different options to choose from, you can be sure to find the perfect car for your needs. Whether you're looking for a compact car for a quick getaway or a larger SUV for a family vacation, the rental car website has you covered. I used my frontend skills to build a responsive website accessible on all types of devices. During the design process i visited different types of websites of the same industry to get inspiration for building a good User Interface that will bring great results for users.
